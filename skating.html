Skating

Public Session

Looking for something to do that is healthy, affordable, and fun for all ages? Then public skating at Halo Rink is the perfect afternoon or night out for you. Whether you are with the family, out with friends, or on a date, come skating and get ready for a lot of laughs and fond memories. Whether you’re just starting out or you’ve been skating for years, we promise that you will enjoy the atmosphere at any one of our public sessions. See More...

We play all your favorite songs, from the latest hits to the classics and encourage you to sing and dance your day or night away. When the lights go off, get ready for “disco time” as our neon party lights get everybody excited! Public sessions are offered almost every afternoon and Saturday night. Watch for promotional skates that often offer discounts and themes. Be sure to take a look at our schedule and take note of the public skate times each day. Also watch out for bonus sessions around holidays, especially Christmas and New Year’s. The group discount of $6.50 per person (including skate rental) is available to parties of 20 or more people ($5.50 for groups of 50 or more). All busy public skate sessions are supervised by our staff of “Skate Guards.” They will be easy to spot, as they skate around in marked, red jackets. They are friendly and eager to answer questions or assist those in need. We also have a Police Officer on hand every Saturday night for added safety and security. Skate aids/walkers are available for rent on a first come, first serve basis. Skaters must be at least 13 years old to come public skating without adult supervision. All skaters are required to follow the public skate rules posted around the building or you may be asked to leave.

Ice skating is a fun activity the whole family can enjoy. Halo Rink facilities have a wide range of rental skates to fit almost any sized foot-from toddlers to adults (up to size 14 available).

Figure Skating

Skating Programs for every level and age!

If you’ve dreamed of being an Olympic ice skater or just enjoy the grace and beauty of the sport as a recreational skater, Halo Rink is the place for you. Halo Rink offers Learn to Skate classes for a variety of ages and skill levels. Please contact us for more information regarding Learn to Skate classes.

Freestyle Sessions

Halo Rink’s Freestyle sessions are completely tailored for Figure skaters and their private ice time! This is specific for anyone to come out and work on any of their moves, jumps, spins etc, without the feel of a Public session crowd! Freestyle Sessions are also perfect for practicing that big routine number, as well as your private lessons with your favorite coaches.

All sessions are subject to change, please make sure to call our pro shop at 000-000-0000 before coming down to verify dates and times. Cost for Freestyle is $13/session. Freestyle session days and times may vary. Please see the schedule for more information.
