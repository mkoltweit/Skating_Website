Hockey

Halo Rink Youth Hockey Leagues are designed to provide your player with the best possible hockey experience while teaching important skills such as teamwork, fair play and building confidence.

Halo Rink Youth Hockey is working as a USA Hockey Facility. League emphasis is on skill development, learning a variety of positions and most importantly, having fun! Our game format will encourage players to have equal ice time in a fun atmosphere.

Are you ready to play the world’s fastest and most exciting team sport?

The philosophy of the Hockey Department is to promote the game for players of all ages and skill levels in a safe and organized environment. From novice to the professional player, we offer ice hockey leagues at all skill levels. We strive to foster camaraderie, friendship and physical fitness while maintaining a fun, enjoyable and exciting atmosphere.

Stick Time

Halo Rink’s Stick Time sessions are completely tailored for hockey players and their ice time! This is specific for anyone to come out and work on any moves and skills. Stick Time sessions are also perfect for your private lessons with your favorite coaches.

All sessions are subject to change, please make sure to call our pro shop at 000-000-0000 before coming down to verify dates and times. Cost for Stick Time is $15/session. Stick Time session days and times may vary. Please see the schedule for more information.
